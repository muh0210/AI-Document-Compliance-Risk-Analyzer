CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date of last signature below.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Consultant may provide advisory services as needed by the Client. The Consultant should endeavor to meet project deadlines and is expected to deliver work of reasonable quality. Services will be performed at the Consultant's discretion, and additional tasks may be added as appropriate.

2. COMPENSATION

Payment for services rendered will be approximately $5,000 per month, payable within a reasonable time after receipt of invoice. The Client might adjust compensation based on performance, and bonuses could be awarded at the Client's sole discretion.

3. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on the effective date and continue indefinitely until terminated by either party. The agreement shall auto-renew annually unless written notice is provided.

4. RESPONSIBILITIES

The Consultant is solely responsible for all deliverables and shall bear full responsibility for any errors, omissions, or deficiencies in the work product. The Consultant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any claims arising from the Consultant's performance.

The Client will endeavor to provide timely feedback but is not obligated to do so within any specific timeframe.

5. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

The Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Client. The Consultant shall have no authority to bind the Client in any manner.

6. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. The Client reserves the right to modify this agreement at any time without notice. Any disputes arising from this Agreement should be resolved through good faith negotiations.

All terms not explicitly defined herein shall be interpreted as the Client deems appropriate.
