FREELANCE WORK AGREEMENT

Date: March 10, 2025

This informal agreement outlines the working relationship between Designer Jane and Startup XYZ.

Work Description:
Jane will do some design work for the startup. Tasks may include logo design, UI mockups, and possibly branding materials. The exact deliverables will be figured out as we go along.

Payment:
Jane might be paid around $3,000 for the project, but the final amount could change depending on how much work is needed. Payment will be made at some point after the work is done, probably within a reasonable timeframe.

Timeline:
The project should be done in approximately 4-6 weeks, give or take. Jane will try her best to meet deadlines but can't make any guarantees. If things take longer than expected, we'll figure it out.

Revisions:
Jane will attempt to accommodate a reasonable number of revisions. What counts as "reasonable" is up for discussion.

Ownership:
The ownership of the designs is to be determined later. Generally, Jane might retain some rights, but the startup could potentially use the designs as needed.

Cancellation:
If either party wants to stop working together, they should probably give some notice. The details of what happens to partial work and partial payment are to be agreed upon if and when that happens.

Other Terms:
Both parties should endeavor to communicate openly. Any issues that come up will hopefully be resolved through good faith discussions. If not, we'll figure something out.

This agreement is based on mutual trust and good vibes. Let's make something great together!
